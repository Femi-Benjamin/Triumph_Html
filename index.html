About

I work at the point where motion design meets product marketing — most of my recent output has been explainer films and launch promos for SaaS and product teams who need a complicated idea to land in under a minute.

That means I don't just animate — I think in terms of what a viewer needs to understand and when, then build the cut, the sound, and the grade to serve that. It's the same instinct I bring whether I'm in After Effects building a sequence frame by frame, or in Resolve shaping the final pace of an edit.

Based in Lagos, working with teams anywhere.
